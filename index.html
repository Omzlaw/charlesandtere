  • In A Relationship

    December 21st, 2020

    We had tried dating around 2016, but it didn’t last long. We ended the relationship in 2017, but somehow we always just were involved in each other’s lives. There were points we decided we weren’t going to hang out again; but Omo, the heart wants what the heart wants. I was the indecisive one at that point, as regards trying to date again; but I guess I was dealing with a few things she had to sort out before actually committing to a relationship. So I genuinely wasn’t ready, or atleast I thought the chapter of Charles and I had been closed. I started to gain perspective and clarity when I actively began working on knowing Abba more, and handling my self esteem issues and the likes. I was thrilled when I got to the point where I could say, if all I have is Jesus, that’s more than enough; so if the relationship didn’t happen, I’d be fine regardless. Anyways in 2021, after my reign of indecisiveness, lol, I was ready to be in a relationship with Charles again, and I was happy with my decision. It wasn’t easy getting to this point, especially with our tribal difficulties, but I’m grateful we’re here now. We don’t have an official date for when our relationship started, but let’s go with December 21st, 2020; our make shift day.
